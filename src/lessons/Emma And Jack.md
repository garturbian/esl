On a warm Saturday morning, the city felt calm and easy. The streets were not too busy, and a soft light came through the windows of small apartments and shops. In one of these apartments, Emma sat by her window with her phone in her hand. She looked outside for a moment, watching people walk by, and then she smiled as she typed a message.

7. What is in Emma’s hand?

10. How does Emma feel when she types the message?
