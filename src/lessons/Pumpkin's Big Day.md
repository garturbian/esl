## Pumpkin's Big Day

Pumpkin was a very cute puppy. He was small and fluffy, like a little cloud! His fur was the color of warm, sweet
pumpkin spice. Pumpkin loved to play.

Every morning, when the sun woke up, Pumpkin woke up too. His tail wagged *thump-thump-thump* against the wooden
floor. "Woof!" he barked, a happy, little sound.

Today was a special day. Today was the day at the big park! His owner, Lily, put a bright red leash on his neck.
Pumpkin wiggled his whole body. He was so excited!

When they walked to the park, Pumpkin saw many smells. *Sniff, sniff*. He smelled grass, and he smelled dogs, and
he smelled sunshine!

The park was big. There were green fields and big, tall trees. Pumpkin saw other puppies! One puppy was brown.
Another puppy was white. They looked like playtime friends!

Pumpkin wagged his tail very fast. He wanted to play! A big, yellow ball rolled near him. *Boing!* Pumpkin jumped
up. He ran fast, chasing the ball. He tumbled a little bit in the grass, but it was okay. Falling was just part of
being a puppy!

Soon, Pumpkin met a friendly puppy. The brown puppy rolled over. Pumpkin barked softly, "Woof?" It was a friendly
greeting. They played together: chasing, tumbling, and laughing with their happy barks.

When Lily called, "Time to go, Pumpkin!", he stopped playing. He yawned a big, puppy yawn. He rested his head on
Lily’s leg. He was tired, but his heart was full of happy memories. He dreamed of chasing yellow balls all night
long. What a wonderful day for a cute puppy!
