In this lesson, we'll learn how to introduce ourselves and others.

### Introducing Yourself:
- My name is...
- I'm from...
- I am a [student/teacher/etc.]

### Introducing Others:
- This is my friend...
- I'd like you to meet...
- Have you met...?

Practice these phrases with someone new today!
