Once, there were three little friends: a baby boy, a puppy, and a kitten. People called them “baby,” “puppy,” and “kitty.”

{% audioPlayer "/audio/wigs-and-twigs_0.mp3" %}

The baby’s name was Harry, the puppy’s name was Wigs, and the kitten’s name was Twigs. The puppy was called Wigs because the hair around his ears and eyes was so curly that it looked like a wig.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/wigs-and-twigs_1.mp3" %}

The kitten was called Twigs because it took many little twigs to keep her out of trouble. They were good friends, but also a bit naughty, and they all lived in the same house.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/wigs-and-twigs_2.mp3" %}

They ran and played all day long. They chased each other around the house until they were too tired and had to rest. Twigs chased Wigs, Wigs chased Twigs, and Harry chased both of them. Sometimes, their mother would chase all three of them.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/wigs-and-twigs_3.mp3" %}

Sometimes Harry would fall and hurt himself. Sometimes Wigs would bite too hard, or Twigs would scratch too deeply.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/wigs-and-twigs_4.mp3" %}

Sometimes Harry would hit Wigs too hard or squeeze Twigs too tightly. Sometimes Twigs would hit Wigs, or Wigs would bite Twigs. So they often got hurt.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/wigs-and-twigs_5.mp3" %}

When Harry was hurt, he would cry, “Oh! Oh!” When Twigs was hurt, she would cry, “Meow! Meow!” When Wigs was hurt, he would cry, “Kee! Kee!” When they were all hurt at the same time and cried together, their voices mixed so much that no one could understand them.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/wigs-and-twigs_6.mp3" %}

Harry slept in a small bed, Wigs slept in a basket, and Twigs slept on a soft mat by the warm chimney.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/wigs-and-twigs_7.mp3" %}

Twigs slept with one eye open to watch Wigs, and Wigs slept with one eye open to watch Twigs. But Harry slept with both eyes closed, like a sensible baby, and did not worry about what anyone else was doing.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/wigs-and-twigs_8.mp3" %}

One day, Harry became sick and could not play. He had to stay in bed all day. Wigs missed his friend, and Twigs looked sad because Wigs was sad.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/wigs-and-twigs_9.mp3" %}

They tried to play together, but they could not. At last, they both went to Harry’s room, where he was fast asleep in bed.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/wigs-and-twigs_10.mp3" %}

Wigs jumped up and curled up beside him on one side, and Twigs jumped up and curled up on the other side. Soon, they were both asleep.  

{% audioPlayer "/audio/wigs-and-twigs_11.mp3" %}

After a while, their mother came into the room and found all three of them sleeping together. Harry in the middle, with Wigs and Twigs close beside him.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/wigs-and-twigs_12.mp3" %}

When Harry woke up, the doctor said he was much better. He smiled and said that maybe Wigs and Twigs had helped him get well. After that, the puppy and the kitten were always called “Dr. Wigs” and “Dr. Twigs.”
