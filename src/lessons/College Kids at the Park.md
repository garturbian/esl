George and his friends went to the park together. They started by playing on the swings. People looked at them because they were college students, not kids. Still, they just wanted to relax and feel like young kids again.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/college-kids-at-the-park_0.mp3" %}

After a while, a mother gently tapped George on the shoulder. She said, “Can you get off the swing?” George looked confused and asked, “Why?” She smiled and said, “My little girl wants to use it.”

{% audioPlayer "/audio/college-kids-at-the-park_1.mp3" %}

George turned and saw a girl, about 10 years old, waiting quietly. He felt a little embarrassed, so he got off the swing and said, “Here, you  can swing now.” His friends laughed at him, but he didn’t mind. Then instead, he went to the slide and had fun on it.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/college-kids-at-the-park_2.mp3" %}

Later, they moved to the monkey bars and started playing again. They were laughing and having a great time when a group of kids came over. The kids also wanted to play, but George and his friends didn’t want to stop because they were enjoying themselves too much.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/college-kids-at-the-park_3.mp3" %}

One boy, around 8 years old, looked at them and said, “You guys are too big for this playground!” George and his friends laughed and kept playing ignoring the boy.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/college-kids-at-the-park_4.mp3" %}

Soon, the park manager walked over and said, “This playground is for children. You need to leave.” This time, George and his friends felt a bit sad because they were having so much fun.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/college-kids-at-the-park_5.mp3" %}

As they were walking out of the park, George noticed a sign. It said, “Adults Only Playground: 18 and older.” He smiled and showed it to his friends. They all looked at each other, laughed, and decided to go there instead. At the new playground, they had an even better time and they felt like young kids again.
