Sally plays basketball. The game is fun. She looks at her teammate. Her teammate passes the ball to her. Sally runs to her team's side of the basketball court. She shoots the ball into the basket. Her team is happy. They hug her. They shout her name. The people watching shout, "Hooray!" 

Suddenly, a dog runs onto the court! The dog steals the basketball. Everyone laughs as the dog runs in circles with the ball. The players try to get the ball back but they cannot. Finally, a little girl calls the dog. The dog drops the ball and runs to her. Everyone cheers again!

# Questions:  

1. What game does Sally play?
2. How is the game?

4. Where does Sally run?

6. How does her team feel?
7. What suddenly happens during the game?
8. What does the dog do?
9. How do people react to the dog?
10. How does the game end?
