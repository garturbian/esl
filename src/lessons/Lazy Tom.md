Tom felt very hungry. But Tom was also very lazy. He did not want to cook food. He did not want to go to a restaurant to eat. He did not even want to heat his leftovers in the microwave. So, Tom had an idea. He decided to get pizza delivered to his house. 

Tom took out his phone and went online. He ordered a small pizza. He picked his favorite toppings. After he finished ordering, he sat on the couch and waited for the pizza. 

Later, Tom heard the doorbell ring. At first, he was too lazy to get up. He waited five minutes. Then he finally opened the door. The pizza smelled so good! Tom felt happy. He paid the delivery man. He even gave him a tip because the man was nice. 

Tom sat back on the couch again. He opened the pizza box and started to eat. The pizza was delicious! He felt full and satisfied. 

Suddenly, there was a knock at the door again. Tom was surprised. He opened the door, and it was a cat! The cat looked hungry too. Tom laughed and shared his pizza with the cat.
