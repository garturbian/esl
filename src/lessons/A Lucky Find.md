![A Lucky Find]({{ '/images/a-lucky-find.png' | url }})

Steven walks around his town on a sunny day. He feels happy as he walks past stores and people. Suddenly, he trips over something on the ground. He looks down and sees a wallet lying there. He picks it up and opens it. Inside, he finds 500 dollars in cash, some credit cards, and a driver’s license. 

{% audioPlayer "/audio/a-lucky-find_0.mp3" %}

Steven knows he must return the wallet to the owner. He walks to his town’s lost and found center and hands it to the worker there. Now, the wallet can be returned.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/a-lucky-find_1.mp3" %}

The next day, Steven receives a thank-you letter in the mail. Inside the letter, there is a gift card and a note from the wallet’s owner saying, “Thank you, Steven, for your kindness\!” Steven smiles, feeling proud and happy for doing the right thing.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/a-lucky-find_2.mp3" %}

# Keywords:

trips \- 絆倒    
He trips over a rock.

cash \- 現金    
I pay with cash at the store.

license \- 執照    
He has a driver’s license.  
lost and found \- 失物招領    
She goes to the lost and found to find her bag.

# Questions:

1\. What does Steven find on the ground?  
2\. How much money is in the wallet?  
3\. What else is in the wallet besides money?  
4\. Where does Steven take the wallet?  
5\. Who does Steven give the wallet to?  
6\. What does Steven receive the next day?  
7\. How does Steven feel after getting the thank-you letter?

# Answer Key:

1\. Steven finds a wallet.  
2\. There is $500 in the wallet.  
3\. There are credit cards and a driver’s license.

5\. Steven gives the wallet to the worker at the lost and found.
