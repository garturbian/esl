![A Day in the Garden](/esl/images/a-day-in-the-garden.png)
The garden is beautiful. The garden is big. There are many flowers. There are pink flowers. There are purple flowers. There are small flowers. There are big flowers. The gardener plants more flowers. There is also a fountain. Kids sit next to it. Kids throw coins into it. Their parents smile.

One day, a small frog jumps out from behind the flowers. The kids see the frog and run to it. They are excited. The frog hops around, and the kids laugh. They throw more coins to see what the frog will do next. The frog jumps into the fountain, and everyone cheers.

# Keywords:  

garden - 花園    
The garden is big and beautiful.  
花園又大又漂亮。    
    
flowers - 花    
There are many flowers in the garden.  
花園裡有很多花。    
    
fountain - 噴泉    
There is a fountain in the garden.  
花園裡有一個噴泉。    
    
coins - 硬幣    
The kids throw coins into the fountain.  
孩子們把硬幣丟進噴泉裡。    
    
excited - 興奮的    
The kids are excited to see the frog.  
孩子們看到青蛙很興奮。

# Questions:  

1. How is the garden?    
2. What kinds of flowers are in the garden?    
3. Who plants more flowers?    
4. Who sits next to the fountain?    
5. What do kids throw into the fountain?    
6. How do the parents feel when the kids throw coins?    
7. What jumps out from behind the flowers?    
8. What do the kids do when they see the frog?    
9. What does the frog do after jumping around?    
10. How do the kids feel when the frog jumps into the fountain?

# Answer Key:

4. Kids sit next to the fountain.    
5. Kids throw coins into the fountain.    
6. Their parents smile.    
7. A small frog jumps out from behind the flowers.    
8. The kids run to the frog and are excited.    
9. The frog jumps into the fountain.    
10. The kids cheer when the frog jumps into the fountain.
