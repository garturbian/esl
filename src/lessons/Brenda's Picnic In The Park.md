Brenda wants to have a picnic. She wakes up early and feels excited. She gets a big basket from the kitchen. She puts healthy sandwiches in the basket. And she adds fruits like apples and bananas. The sandwiches are tasty. She wants to eat outside in the park. 

Brenda drives to the park. The park is beautiful. There are green trees and colorful flowers. She finds a nice spot under a big tree. She lays out a soft blanket on the grass. She smiles and thinks about her picnic. 

Suddenly, she hears a sound. It is raining! She looks up and sees dark clouds. She quickly folds the blanket. She puts the food back in the basket. Then, she notices a small bird sitting nearby. The bird looks wet and cold. 

Brenda feels sad for the bird. She takes out a piece of bread from her basket. She throws it gently to the bird. The bird flies to the bread and eats it. Brenda laughs. She says, "Now we can both enjoy lunch!" To her surprise, the bird starts to sing a happy song. Brenda smiles widely. She says, "This is the best picnic ever, even in the rain!"
