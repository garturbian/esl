Brenda wants to have a picnic. She wakes up early and feels excited. She gets a big basket from the kitchen. She puts healthy sandwiches in the basket. And she adds fruits like apples and bananas. The sandwiches are tasty. She wants to eat outside in the park. 

# Questions.

1. Where does Brenda want to go?

2. Why does she wake up early?

3. How does she feel in the morning?

4. What does she get from the kitchen?

5. What does she put in the basket?

6. What fruits does she add?

7. Are the sandwiches healthy or unhealthy?

8. Where does she want to eat?

{% audioPlayer "/audio/brendas-picnic-in-the-park-2.mp3" %}

Brenda drives to the park. The park is beautiful. There are green trees and colorful flowers. She finds a nice spot under a big tree. She lays out a soft blanket on the grass. She smiles and thinks about her picnic. 

# Questions.

1. How does Brenda go to the park?

2. How is the park?

3. What colors are the trees and flowers?

4. Where does she find a spot?

5. What does she put on the grass?

6. Is the blanket soft or hard?

7. What does she think about?

{% audioPlayer "/audio/brendas-picnic-in-the-park-3.mp3" %}

Suddenly, she hears a sound. It is raining\! She looks up and sees dark clouds. She quickly folds the blanket. She puts the food back in the basket. Then, she notices a small bird sitting nearby. The bird looks wet and cold. 

# Questions.

1. What does Brenda hear?

2. What is the weather like?

3. What does she see in the sky?

4. What does she do with the blanket?

5. What does she do with the food?

6. What animal does she notice?

7. Where is the bird sitting?

8. How does the bird look?

{% audioPlayer "/audio/brendas-picnic-in-the-park-4.mp3" %}

Brenda feels sad for the bird. She takes out a piece of bread from her basket. She throws it gently to the bird. The bird flies to the bread and eats it. Brenda laughs. She says, "Now we can both enjoy lunch\!" To her surprise , the bird starts to sing a happy song. Brenda smiles widely. She says, "This is the best picnic ever, even in the rain\!"

# Questions.

1. Why does Brenda feel sad?

2. What does she take out of her basket?

3. How does she throw the bread?

4. What does the bird do after that?

5. Why does Brenda laugh?

6. What does she say about lunch?

7. What does the bird do to surprise her?

8. How does Brenda feel at the end?

9. Is the picnic perfect weather?

10. Do you think she still has a good picnic? Why?

 

# Keywords.

1\. Basket \- 籃子    
   She carries a basket with food to the park.  
她帶著一個帶食物的籃子到公園。

2\. Sandwiches \- 三明治    
   She makes delicious sandwiches for her picnic.  
她為自己的野餐製作美味的三明治。

3\. Healthy \- 健康的    
   The healthy fruits are good for her.  
健康的水果對她有好處。

4\. Tasty \- 美味的    
   The sandwiches taste very tasty.   
三明治的味道非常美味。

5\. Blanket \- 毛毯    
   She lays the blanket on the grass to sit on.  
她把毯子放在草地上。

6\. Raining \- 下雨    
   It is raining, so she has to pack her food.  
下雨了，所以她必須打包食物。

7\. Bird \- 鳥    
   A small bird sits on the branch near her.  
一隻小鳥坐在她附近的樹枝上。

8\. Cold \- 冷的    
   The bird looks cold in the rain.  
鳥在雨中看起來很冷。

9\. Throw \- 扔    
   She throws a piece of bread to the bird.  
她把一塊麵包扔給那隻鳥。

10\. Sing \- 唱    
   The bird sings a happy song after it eats.  
鳥兒吃完後，鳥兒唱了一首快樂的歌。
