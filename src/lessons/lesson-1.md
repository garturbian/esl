In this lesson, we'll learn some of the most common ways to say "Hello" in English!

### Formal Greetings:
- Hello
- Good morning
- Good afternoon
- Good evening

### Informal Greetings:
- Hi
- Hey
- What's up?
- How's it going?

Try practicing these with a partner or in front of a mirror!
