Virginia wanted to hang out with her friend Claudia. They had not seen each other in four years. Virginia was excited to see her again. She called Claudia on the phone. "Hey, it's me, Ginny!" she said. Claudia sounded happy. "Wow! I have not seen you for a long time," Claudia replied. Virginia asked, "Want to get lunch at IHOP?" Claudia said, "I'm sorry, I have class." Virginia felt sad. They said good-bye and hung up the phone.

Virginia decided to go to IHOP with another friend. Before she went inside the restaurant, she saw Claudia walk by. Virginia was surprised to see her. "I thought you had class," Virginia said. Claudia looked a little nervous. "Uh… I... did I say that?" she replied. Virginia was not happy. She said, "Forget it. I would never want to hang out with a liar." Then she walked inside the restaurant.

# Keywords:

hang out - 出去玩  

Virginia wanted to hang out with Claudia after a long time.

弗吉尼亞州想在很長一段時間後與克勞迪婭閒逛。

lunch - 午餐  

Virginia asked Claudia to get lunch at IHOP.

弗吉尼亞要求克勞迪婭在IHOP吃午餐。

IHOP  The International House of Pancakes

國際煎餅之家

class - 課程  

Claudia told Virginia that she had class.

克勞迪婭告訴弗吉尼亞，她上課了。

sad - 悲傷的  

Virginia felt sad when Claudia said no.

當克勞迪婭拒絕時，弗吉尼亞感到難過。

surprised - 驚訝的  

Virginia was surprised to see Claudia walking by.

弗吉尼亞很驚訝地看到克勞迪婭走過。

lied - 撒謊  

Virginia thought Claudia had lied about her class.

弗吉尼亞以為克勞迪婭對她的班級說謊。

forget it - 算了  

Virginia said, "Forget it," because she was angry.

弗吉尼亞說：“忘了”，因為她很生氣。

liar - 說謊者  

Virginia did not want to be friends with a liar.

弗吉尼亞不想和騙子成為朋友。

call - 打電話  

Virginia called Claudia to talk about lunch.

弗吉尼亞打電話給克勞迪婭談論午餐。

friend - 朋友  

Claudia is Virginia's friend who she wanted to see.

克勞迪婭（Claudia）是弗吉尼亞州的朋友，她想看。
