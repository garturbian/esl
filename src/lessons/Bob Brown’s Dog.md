![Bob Brown’s Dog]({{ '/images/bob-browns-dog.png' | url }})

Bob Brown had a big, beautiful dog named Rover. They were best friends and spent almost all their time playing together.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/bob-browns-dog_0.mp3" %}

When Bob turned six, he had to start school. He was excited, but also a little sad because he had to leave Rover at home. Before he left, he hugged Rover and whispered something in his ear. Then he ran off to school happily, without telling anyone what he said.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/bob-browns-dog_1.mp3" %}

About an hour after school started, a large dog appeared at the front door. When no one was looking, he quietly slipped inside. He walked through the hallways, went up and down the stairs, and sniffed everything.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/bob-browns-dog_2.mp3" %}

No one saw him because all the teachers and students were busy in their classrooms. Then Rover found a small cap and coat he recognized. “These belong to my boy!” he seemed to think.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/bob-browns-dog_3.mp3" %}

He picked them up in his mouth and walked into a classroom where he saw Bob. His tail wagged as he happily walked up to him.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/bob-browns-dog_4.mp3" %}

Rover dropped the cap and coat into Bob’s lap. Then he gently grabbed Bob’s pants with his teeth and started pulling. It was his way of saying, “Come on, Bob! Let’s go home!”

{% audioPlayer "/audio/bob-browns-dog_5.mp3" %}

Bob was so happy to see Rover that he forgot he was in class. He hugged him and said out loud, “I knew you would come for me, you good old dog!” All the children laughed, and even the teacher smiled. Then she told Bob he could take his dog home.
