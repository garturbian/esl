![The Sweet Mistake]({{ '/images/the-sweet-mistake.png' | url }})

Susie loves eating chocolate, especially chocolate with caramel. One day, her mom gives her a chocolate bar. Susie is excited and takes a big bite. The chocolate is delicious, but it is very sweet. After eating a few more bites, she starts feeling thirsty. 

{% audioPlayer "/audio/the-sweet-mistake1.mp3" %}

"Mom, can you get me a drink?" Susie asks. Her mom hands her a drink, and Susie drinks it quickly. But when she tastes it, she frowns. It is chocolate milk!  "Too sweet!" Susie complains loudly. "You didn't say what kind of drink you wanted," her mom says with a smile. 

{% audioPlayer "/audio/the-sweet-mistake2.mp3" %}

Susie gets up, grabs a water bottle from the kitchen, and drinks a lot of water. "Much better," she says. She goes back to the table and continues eating her chocolate bar.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/the-sweet-mistake3.mp3" %}

Suddenly , Susie feels a little funny. "I ate too much chocolate," she says, holding her stomach. Her mom looks at her and says, "Maybe it's time for a break , Susie." Susie laughs and agrees. "No more chocolate for me today!"
