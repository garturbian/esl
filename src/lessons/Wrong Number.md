One evening, Sara sat on her couch watching her favorite TV show. Suddenly, her phone rang. She looked at the number, but she did not know it.

"Hello?" Sara said.

"Hi, is this Tom?" a man asked.

"No, sorry, you have the wrong number," Sara said politely.

The man said sorry and hung up. Sara smiled and went back to watching TV. A few minutes later, her phone rang again. It was the same number.

"Hello?" Sara answered, feeling curious.

"Tom, it's really important. I need to talk to you!" the man said, sounding worried.

Sara sighed. "I'm not Tom. Please stop calling."

"I'm sorry, I just really need to find him," the man said and hung up quickly. Sara thought it was strange, but she did not worry much.

Later that night, her phone rang again, but this time it was an unknown number.

"Hello?" Sara said.

The voice on the other end was deep and serious. "This is Tom. Who has been looking for me?"

Sara froze.

It starts with Sarah, the main character, getting a call from an unknown number. The person asks for a man named Tom, and the call ends. It seems like nothing important happened. But the caller keeps calling back, making the situation feel strange. Later, Sarah gets another call from a man who says he is Tom, and the story changes quickly. It turns out the caller is not who he says he is, and the story ends suddenly, leaving readers with questions and a feeling of discomfort. The ending makes readers think about what happened, focusing on themes of fear, uncertainty, and mistaken identity.
