Veronica is 21 years old, but she still doesn’t have a driver’s license. Her friends often tease her about it, but she doesn’t mind. Veronica loves walking. She can walk fast, and she enjoys walking for long hours. Every day, she walks to school, to work, and even to the market when she needs groceries. She doesn’t get tired easily and finds walking very relaxing. It makes her feel calm and happy.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/veronicas-choice2.mp3" %}

Running, however, is not the same for Veronica. She does not like running at all. Running makes her feel tired very quickly, and her stomach always hurts when she runs. Even though her new job is far away, Veronica doesn’t plan to buy a car. She doesn’t even want to learn how to drive. Instead, she plans to take the bus to work.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/veronicas-choice3.mp3" %}

One day, after her first week at the new job, Veronica was waiting at the bus stop. The bus was late, and it started to rain. She didn’t have an umbrella, so she decided to run home, even though she hated running. As she ran, something strange happened. Veronica realized that she was enjoying it. She ran faster and faster, and for the first time, her stomach didn’t hurt.

{% audioPlayer "/audio/veronicas-choice4.mp3" %}

When she finally reached home, she was smiling. “Maybe running isn’t so bad after all,” she thought. From that day, Veronica started running every morning before work. She didn’t need a car or a bus anymore—she loved running to work instead!

# Questions:  

1. How old is Veronica?  
2. Does Veronica have a driver’s license?  
3. What do Veronica’s friends do because she doesn’t have a license?  
4. How does Veronica feel about walking?  
5. How does Veronica feel about running?  
6. What does Veronica plan to do for her new job that is far away?  
7. Why did Veronica start running when she was waiting for the bus?  
8. How did Veronica feel while running in the rain?  
9. What surprised Veronica after she finished running?  
10. What did Veronica start doing every morning after her first run?

# Answer Key:  

1. Veronica is 21 years old.  
2. No, she doesn’t have a driver’s license.  
3. They tease her.  
4. She loves walking; it is relaxing for her.  
5. She doesn’t like running because it makes her tired, and her stomach hurts.  
6. She plans to take the bus.  
7. She started running because the bus was late and it started to rain.  
8. She felt good and realized she enjoyed running.  
9. She was surprised that her stomach didn’t hurt and that she enjoyed running.  
10. She started running to work every morning.
