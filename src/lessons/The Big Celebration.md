Mary's basketball team won a big game. They want to celebrate. They have to buy some things for the   celebration. Patricia goes to the bakery shop. She buys a cake shaped like a basketball. Linda buys some decorations and Sally buys balloons. All the team members meet at Mary's house for the party. They have fun celebrating.

# Questions:

1. Who won a big game?  
2. What sport does Mary play?

3. Why do they want to celebrate?

4. Who goes to the bakery shop?

5. What does Patricia buy?

6. What shape is the cake?

7. Who buys decorations?

8. Who buys balloons?

9. Where do the team members meet?

10. What do they do at Mary’s house?

11. Do you like parties? Why or why not?

{% audioPlayer "/audio/the-big-celebration-2.mp3" %}

Suddenly, they hear a loud noise outside. They look out the window. A noisy parade is going passed in the street! The team runs outside to see it. They cheer with the crowd. They forget about the party for a moment. After the parade ends, they go back into the house and finish their celebration with cake and balloons.

# Questions:

1. What do they hear outside?

2. Is the noise loud or quiet?

3. Where do they look?

4. What is going by outside in the street?

5. Is the parade quiet or noisy?

6. What do the team members do?

7. Who do they cheer with?

8. Do they forget about the party?

9. For how long do they forget the party?

10. What do they do after the parade ends?
