Mary's basketball team won a big game. They want to celebrate. They have to buy some things for the celebration. Patricia goes to the bakery shop. She buys a cake shaped like a basketball. Linda buys some decorations and Sally buys balloons. All the team members meet at Mary's house for the party. They have fun celebrating.

Suddenly, they hear a loud noise outside. They look out the window. A noisy parade is going past in the street! The team runs outside to see it. They cheer with the crowd. They forget about the party for a moment. After the parade ends, they go back into the house and finish their celebration with cake and balloons.

# Keywords:  

- celebrate - 慶祝
- bakery - 麵包店
- decorations - 裝飾品
- balloons - 氣球
- parade - 遊行

# Questions:

1. Who won a big game?  
2. Why do they want to celebrate?
3. What does Patricia buy from the bakery?
4. What shape is the cake?
5. Who buys decorations and balloons?
6. Where do the team members meet?
7. What do they hear outside during the party?
8. What is going by in the street?
9. What do the team members do when they see the parade?
10. What do they do after the parade ends?

# Answer Key:

1. Mary's basketball team won.
2. They want to celebrate their big win.
3. Patricia buys a basketball-shaped cake.
4. The cake is shaped like a basketball.
5. Linda buys decorations and Sally buys balloons.
6. They meet at Mary's house.
7. They hear a loud noise outside.
8. A noisy parade is going past.
9. They run outside and cheer with the crowd.
10. They go back inside and finish their celebration.
