Mary goes to the store. The store is very big. It has many aisles and many products. The store sells bakery goods, fruits, vegetables, and drinks. Mary loves to explore the bakery section. She enjoys the sweet smell of fresh bread and pastries.

1: Where does Mary go?  
2: Is the store small or big?  
3: What does the store sell?  
4: Can you name two foods the store sells?  
5: Which part of the store does Mary like?  
6: What smell does Mary enjoy in the bakery section?  
7: Do you like the smell of fresh bread? Why?  
8: Do you like to go to big stores? Why or why not?  

{% audioPlayer "/audio/mary-makes-muffins2.mp3" %}

As she walks through the bakery, Mary sees a big carrot cake. It looks very pretty with white icing and orange carrots on top. But Mary does not like carrots. She shakes her head and moves on.


1: What does Mary see in the bakery?  
2: What kind of cake is it?  
3: What color is the icing on the cake?  
4: What is on top of the cake?  
5: Does Mary like carrots?  
6: What does Mary do when she sees the cake?  
7: Does she buy the cake or move on?  
8: Do you like carrot cake? Why or why not?  

{% audioPlayer "/audio/mary-makes-muffins3.mp3" %}

Next, she sees a banana nut muffin. It looks good and smells sweet. The muffin has nuts on top and a golden color. It costs two dollars. Mary decides to buy it. She pays the cashier and gets her muffin in a paper bag.

1: What does Mary see next?  
2: How does the muffin smell?  
3: What is on top of the muffin?  
4: What color is the muffin?  
5: How much does the muffin cost?  
6: Does Mary buy the muffin?  
7: Who does Mary pay?  
8: What does the cashier give Mary?  
9: Is the muffin in a paper bag or a plastic bag?  
10: Do you like banana muffins? Why or why not?  

{% audioPlayer "/audio/mary-makes-muffins4.mp3" %}

Mary walks outside and sits on a bench. She takes the wrapper off the muffin. She takes a big bite. It is crunchy on the outside. It is soft on the inside. Mary smiles and thinks it is delicious.

1: Where does Mary sit?  
2: Is the bench inside or outside?  
3: What does Mary take off the muffin?  
4: Does she take a small bite or a big bite?  
5: Is the muffin crunchy or soft on the outside?  
6: Is the inside crunchy or soft?  
7: Does Mary smile?  
8: Does Mary think the muffin is delicious?  
9: Do you like muffins?  
10: Where do you like to sit and eat?  

{% audioPlayer "/audio/mary-makes-muffins5.mp3" %}

Suddenly, a squirrel runs up to her. The squirrel looks very interested in the muffin. Mary laughs and decides to share a little bit. She breaks off a small piece and gives it to the squirrel. To her surprise, the squirrel takes the piece and jumps up on her lap. It seems to say thank you! Mary feels happy to share her treat. She says, "This is the best muffin I have ever had!"

1: Who comes to Mary suddenly?  
2: What does the squirrel like?  
3: What does Mary do with the muffin?  
4: How much of the muffin does Mary give to the squirrel?  
5: Where does the squirrel go after taking the muffin?  
6: How does the squirrel act after getting the muffin?  
7: How does Mary feel after sharing?  
8: What does Mary say about the muffin?  
9: Why does Mary laugh?  
10: Do you think Mary will share with the squirrel again?  


# Keywords:

1. Store - 商店  
   Mary goes to the store to buy some food.  
瑪麗去商店買了一些食物。

2. Bakery - 麵包店  
   The bakery sells fresh bread and sweet treats.  
麵包店出售新鮮的麵包和甜食。

3. Carrot - 胡蘿蔔  
   The carrot cake has orange carrots on top.  
胡蘿蔔蛋糕的頂部有橙色胡蘿蔔.

4. Muffin - 馬芬  
   Mary buys a banana nut muffin from the bakery.  
瑪麗從麵包店購買香蕉堅果鬆餅。

5. Costs - 價格  
   The muffin costs two dollars.  
鬆餅的價格為兩美元。

6. Wrapper - 包裝紙  
   She takes the wrapper off before eating.  
她在進食前將包裝紙摘下。

7. Crunchy - 酥脆的  
   The outside of the muffin is crunchy and delicious.  
鬆餅的外部脆脆可口。

8. Soft - 軟的  
   The inside of the muffin is soft and sweet.  
鬆餅的內部柔軟又甜。

9. Squirrel - 松鼠  
   A little squirrel runs up to Mary for food.  
一點點松鼠跑到瑪麗吃食物。

10. Share - 分享  
   Mary decides to share a piece of muffin with the squirrel.  
瑪麗決定與松鼠分享一塊鬆餅.

# Questions:

1. What does Mary see in the store?  
2. What does Mary think about the carrot cake?  
3. How much does the banana nut muffin cost?  
4. Where does Mary sit to eat her muffin?  
5. What happens when Mary takes a bite of the muffin?  
6. What does the squirrel do when it sees Mary?  
7. How does Mary feel after sharing her muffin?  
<br><br>
# Answers:

1. Mary sees a carrot cake and a banana nut muffin in the store.  
2. Mary does not like the carrot cake because it has carrots.  
3. The banana nut muffin costs two dollars.  
4. Mary sits on a bench outside to eat her muffin.  
5. When Mary takes a bite of the muffin, she finds it crunchy 脆脆的 on the outside and soft on the inside.  
6. The squirrel runs up to Mary and looks interested in her muffin.  
7. Mary feels happy after sharing her muffin with the squirrel.
