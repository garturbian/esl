I created these lessons for my private students who speak Mandarin, and now I am happy to share them with everyone. This site is for children and adults, beginners and advanced learners. We focus on high-frequency words, listening practice, and shadow reading, so you do not just read English, you truly understand it and use it. In the near future, you will also be able to use an AI tool that helps you correct your pronunciation and speak more confidently. Practice and learn a little every day, and enjoy watching your English grow.


我為說中文的私人學生設計了這些課程，現在很高興把它們分享給大家。這個網站適合兒童和成人，無論是初學者還是進階學習者。我們專注於高頻單字、聽力練習和跟讀練習，讓你不只是閱讀英文，而是真正理解並運用它。未來你也可以使用 AI 工具來幫助你修正發音，讓你說得更有自信。每天練習並學習一點點，享受看著自己的英文慢慢成長。
